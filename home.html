About Us

The Candelaria's Top Birthing Home

Princess Anne Kristel Birthing Home,

established in 2018, is a trusted Pregnancy Care Center located at 274 MGR Subdivision, Malabanban Norte, Candelaria, Quezon. Our birthing home is dedicated to providing exceptional and compassionate care to expectant mothers and their families, ensuring a safe and comfortable environment for childbirth.

Care and Love

Whether you are a first-time mother or adding a new member to your family, Princess Anne Kristel Birthing Home is here to provide you with the care and support you need. We look forward to being a part of your journey to motherhood.

Pre/Post - Natal Check-Up

Early screening to assess and ensure your newborn's hearing capabilities for prompt intervention if needed.

Newborn Screening Test

Essential tests to detect rare but serious conditions in newborns, ensuring early treatment and care.
